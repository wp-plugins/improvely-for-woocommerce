This plugin integrates Improvely's marketing analytics platform with your WordPress store powered by WooCommerce.

== Description ==

This plugin adds Improvely's powerful ad tracking service to your WooCommerce-powered store. It will automatically record every visit to your site and identify the source of every purchase made. 

Require an active <a href="http://www.improvely.com">Improvely</a> account and <a href="http://www.woocommerce.com">WooCommerce</a> to install.

== Installation ==

1. Enter your Improvely account details from the Improvely page in your Settings menu.
